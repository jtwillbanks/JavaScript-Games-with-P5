Maze

Guide the green block through the maze and reach the end!

Don't touch the walls! They are dangerous!!

Complete all 3 levels to win the game!

How to Play

- Move the green block around the maze and reach the end!

- Hitting any wall will reduce your life count by 1!

- Losing all 3 lives will send you to the previous level!

Controls

W or Up-Arrow - Moves Block Upward

A or Left-Arrow - Moves Block Leftward

S or Down-Arrow - Moves Block Downward

D or Right-Arrow - Moves Block Rightward
