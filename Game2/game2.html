Pac-Man

Guide Pac-Man through a dangerous maze filled with ghost
and collect all the dots in the maze to win!

How to Play

- Move Pac-Man Around and collect the Dots!

- Avoid the ghost around the map!

- Collect all dots to win!

- The side tunnels are only moveable by Pac-Man

Controls

W or Up-Arrow - Moves Pac-Man Upward

A or Left-Arrow - Moves Pac-Man Leftward

S or Down-Arrow - Moves Pac-Man Downward

D or Right-Arrow - Moves Pac-Man Rightward
