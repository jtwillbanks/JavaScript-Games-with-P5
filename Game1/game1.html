Snake

After a long day of coding, Derrick is exhausted and needs a break from the world of text editing.
What better snack than an In-N-Out Double-Double???
Help Derrick achieve conquering his hunger by guiding him to the delicious snack!

How to Play

- Move the snake and collect the double-doubles!

- Each double-double collected increases your size by 1!

- Hitting the edge or yourself will cause you to lose!

Controls

W or Up-Arrow - Moves Snake Upward

A or Left-Arrow - Moves Snake Leftward

S or Down-Arrow - Moves Snake Downward

D or Right-Arrow - Moves Snake Rightward

ESC - Pauses and UnPauses the game
