Racer

Race your way through waves of ruthless traffic!
Avoid colliding with the cars or the sides of the screen

How to Play

- Move Left/Right to avoid the incoming cars!

- Score Increases by 20 for each new wave of cars!

- Hitting the edge or other cars will cause you to lose!

Controls

A or Left-Arrow - Moves Car Leftward

D or Right-Arrow - Moves Car Rightward

ESC - Pauses and UnPauses the game
